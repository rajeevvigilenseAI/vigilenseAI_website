<h2>Common Questions About 24/7 Threat Monitoring</h2>

<h3>How does 24/7 threat monitoring prevent data breaches?</h3>
<p>Continuous monitoring ensures that anomalies—such as unauthorized logins or unusual data transfers—are flagged instantly. By using AI to triage alerts 24/7, threats are contained before they can escalate into full-scale breaches.</p>

<h3>What is the difference between MDR and traditional threat monitoring?</h3>
<p>Traditional monitoring often just alerts you to a problem. Managed Detection and Response (MDR) goes a step further by actively investigating the alert and taking automated or guided actions to stop the attack.</p>

<h3>Why do midsize businesses need AI-powered security?</h3>
<p>Midsize firms lack the budget for massive human teams. AI scales your security by correlating data across 50+ sources in minutes, doing the work that would take a human analyst hours to complete.</p>

<h3>How much do managed detection and response services cost?</h3>
<p>Traditional MDR providers charge per gigabyte of data ingested into their cloud. Vigilense AI changes the math by querying your data where it lives, offering a more predictable and lower total cost of ownership.</p>

<h3>Can threat monitoring work with Snowflake or AWS S3?</h3>
<p>Yes. Modern solutions like Vigilense AI are built to work on top of your existing infrastructure, including Snowflake, Elasticsearch, OpenSearch, S3, and BigQuery, with zero data movement required.</p>

<h3>What are the benefits of keeping security data in your own infrastructure?</h3>
<p>Keeping data in your own infrastructure ensures compliance, reduces latency, eliminates egress/ingestion fees, and prevents vendor lock-in, giving you full sovereignty over your security logs.</p>
</section>
