<section>
<h2>How to Implement AI Security Monitoring (5 Steps)</h2>

<div class="step-guide">
<div class="step-title"><span class="step-number">1</span> Identify Your Data Sources</div>
<p>Start by mapping where your critical business data lives. For most midsize organizations, this includes endpoints, identity providers (like Okta), and cloud storage like Snowflake, S3, or BigQuery.</p>
<ul class="action-items">
<li>Audit cloud storage providers</li>
<li>List all SaaS identity platforms</li>
<li>Identify key network egress points</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="step-guide">
<div class="step-title"><span class="step-number">2</span> Connect Your Existing Infrastructure</div>
<p>Instead of building a new silo, connect your security monitoring tool directly to your data. Vigilense AI queries your data where it already lives, requiring no movement of sensitive logs.</p>
<ul class="action-items">
<li>Configure API access for AI agents</li>
<li>Verify read-only permissions for security layers</li>
<li>Test connectivity to Snowflake or OpenSearch</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="step-guide">
<div class="step-title"><span class="step-number">3</span> Automate Alert Triage</div>
<p>Deploy AI models to monitor your environment 24/7. Every alert should be triaged automatically to separate background noise from actual threats, reducing "alert fatigue" for your IT team.</p>
<ul class="action-items">
<li>Set baseline behavioral norms</li>
<li>Enable 50+ source correlation</li>
<li>Configure automated anomaly detection</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="step-guide">
<div class="step-title"><span class="step-number">4</span> Establish Response Protocols</div>
<p>Determine which threats should be contained automatically. For example, an identity theft attempt can trigger an automatic account lockout, while a suspicious file might just create a high-priority ticket.</p>
<ul class="action-items">
<li>Define automated containment rules</li>
<li>Set up notification channels (Slack/Email)</li>
<li>Establish human-in-the-loop triggers</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="step-guide">
<div class="step-title"><span class="step-number">5</span> Continuous Compliance &amp; Auditing</div>
<p>Use the monitoring platform to maintain a full audit trail. This is critical for SOC2, HIPAA, or GDPR compliance, proving that every incident was investigated and resolved.</p>
<ul class="action-items">
<li>Generate weekly compliance reports</li>
<li>Review AI investigation summaries</li>
<li>Perform quarterly security posture audits</li>
</ul>
</div>
