Pricing FAQ

Is there really no upfront cost?
Correct. We deploy the full Sovereign SOC platform - BYODb SIEM and AI SOC Analyst - at our expense during the pilot. You pay nothing until we demonstrate measurable results.
What happens after the pilot?
After the pilot, we review results together. If the outcomes meet your expectations, we move to a subscription. If not, you walk away with zero obligation. No lock-in, no penalties.
How is pricing structured?
Pricing starts with outcomes: you don't pay until the pilot proves value. Post-pilot, pricing is usage-based - a platform subscription that scales with your security workload (investigations handled, detections running), not your raw data volume. Zero per-GB ingestion fees. Your BYODb infrastructure costs are pass-through to your own cloud provider. Use our Cost Simulator to estimate your savings, or contact sales@vigilense.ai for a custom quote tailored to your environment.
Are there any hidden fees?
No. There are no ingestion fees, no data transfer fees, no storage surcharges, and no surprise overages. Your data stays in your databases - we never move or copy it.
How long is the pilot?
Typical pilots run 2-4 weeks, enough time to connect your databases, deploy the AI analyst, and measure results. Deployment itself takes days, not months.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. There are no long-term commitments. You can cancel at any time with no penalties. We believe in earning your business through results, not contracts.
