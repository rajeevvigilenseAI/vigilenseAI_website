Pricing FAQ

How is pricing structured?
Vigilense AI is priced per endpoint per month with zero ingestion fees. Your bill is based on endpoint count, not data volume. Talk to our team or email sales@vigilense.ai for a tailored quote based on your environment.
Is there really no upfront cost?
Correct. The pilot is free. We deploy the AI SOC analyst and BYODb SIEM at our expense, connect to your environment, and run live investigations. You pay nothing during the pilot.
Are there any ingestion or data volume fees?
No. Zero per-GB ingestion fees, zero data transfer fees, zero storage surcharges. Your log volume does not affect your bill. We query your data in place rather than ingesting it into a proprietary store.
What happens after the pilot?
We review the pilot results together: investigations completed, true positives caught, false positives eliminated, hours saved. If you want to continue, we move you onto a contract that fits your environment. If not, you walk away with zero obligation, your data, and the audit history. No lock-in. No penalties.
How long is the pilot?
Typically 30 days. Deployment itself takes about 5 business days for most environments (read-only connector, detection tuning, shadow mode, go-live). The remaining time is for you to see investigations in action against real alerts and measure outcomes against your current baseline.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Because your data never moves to us, leaving means simply disconnecting the read-only connector. You keep your data, your detections, and your audit history. Talk to our team about contract flexibility.
