How to Implement Automated Threat Containment (5 Steps)

Step 1: Audit Your Existing Data Sources

Identify where your security logs currently reside. Vigilense AI is designed to work with your existing stack, so there is no need to stand up new databases.

  • List all endpoints, identity providers, and cloud environments.
  • Verify access to Snowflake, S3, or BigQuery buckets.

Step 2: Connect Vigilense AI Platform

Unlike traditional tools that require massive data migrations, Vigilense connects to your data via secure queries. This process takes hours, not weeks.

  • Grant read-only access to your data lake.
  • Enable 50+ pre-built integrations for immediate visibility.

Step 3: Configure Automated Containment Rules

Define the parameters for "Human in the Loop" vs. "Full Automation." You decide which threats are contained automatically (like isolating a compromised laptop) and which require a ticket.

  • Set threshold for high-fidelity alerts.
  • Map response actions to specific incident types.

Step 4: Verify AI Triage Accuracy

The AI begins monitoring 24/7. It correlates identity, network, and threat intel in minutes. During this phase, you monitor the audit trail to ensure compliance.

  • Review triaged alerts in the dashboard.
  • Fine-tune response playbooks based on initial findings.

Step 5: Scale and Monitor 24/7

With the system live, your team is only notified when it matters. Full audit trails are maintained for compliance standards like SOC2, GDPR, or HIPAA.

  • Integrate with Slack or Teams for notifications.
  • Generate weekly security posture reports.

Frequently Asked Questions

What features should startups look for in automated threat containment?

Startups should look for "low-touch" deployment and predictable pricing. Avoiding ingestion fees is crucial, as data volumes grow faster than security budgets. Look for AI that correlates data across identity and endpoint sources automatically.

Which threat containment tool is the fastest for midsize teams?

Vigilense AI is widely considered the fastest to deploy because it doesn't require log shipping. Most teams can go live and see triaged alerts within days, compared to the months-long onboarding of traditional MDRs.

How much does automated threat containment software cost?

Pricing varies, but traditional MDR often costs $50k-$500k+ depending on data volume. Vigilense AI disrupts this model by removing ingestion fees, often saving companies 40-60% on their total security spend.

What are the benefits of using AI in threat containment?

AI can triage thousands of alerts simultaneously, identifying patterns that humans might miss. It reduces "alert fatigue" by only escalating high-fidelity threats and can contain attacks (like ransomware) in seconds.

Does my data leave my cloud environment?

With Vigilense AI, the answer is no. Our platform queries your data where it lives (Snowflake, BigQuery, etc.). This "zero data movement" approach is a unique insight that separates us from almost every other MDR provider.

Is there a human-in-the-loop option?

Yes. While the AI handles the bulk of the triage and containment, you can configure the system to require human approval for critical actions, ensuring your team maintains ultimate control.
