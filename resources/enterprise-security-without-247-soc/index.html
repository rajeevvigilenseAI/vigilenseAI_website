How to Implement AI SOC Capabilities (5 Steps)

Step 1: Connect Your Existing Data Infrastructure

Instead of creating new log silos, connect Vigilense to your current data lakes like Snowflake, Elasticsearch, OpenSearch, S3, or BigQuery. This ensures zero data movement and maintains full compliance.

  • Audit existing log sources (Endpoint, Cloud, Identity)
  • Grant read-only access to Vigilense AI
Step 2: Map Your Environment

The AI scans your infrastructure to understand what "normal" looks like. It identifies critical assets, user identities, and network patterns unique to your organization.

  • Define high-value targets and admin accounts
  • Configure 50+ integrated threat intelligence feeds
Step 3: Activate 24/7 AI Triage

Once connected, the AI begins triaging every alert in real-time. Unlike humans who get "alert fatigue," the AI investigates every anomaly across your entire stack simultaneously.

  • Set automation thresholds for low-risk alerts
  • Enable cross-source correlation (e.g., Identity + Network)
Step 4: Configure Automated Response Playbooks

Define how the system should react when a definitive threat is found. This could include isolating a compromised host, revoking an identity token, or blocking a malicious IP.

  • Review pre-built containment playbooks
  • Assign "Human-in-the-Loop" triggers for critical actions
Step 5: Monitor & Continuous Improvement

Use the Vigilense dashboard to review incident timelines and compliance reports. The AI learns from every interaction, refining its detection accuracy over time.

  • Schedule weekly executive security summaries
  • Review automated audit trails for regulatory compliance

Frequently Asked Questions

Which project management tool is the fastest for security teams?

For security teams, the fastest "tool" isn't a task manager, it's an automated workflow engine. Vigilense AI functions as an autonomous SOC manager, triaging alerts in seconds that would take a human analyst 30-60 minutes to investigate.

How much does a midsize security operations center cost?

A traditional 24/7 SOC requires at least 8-12 full-time analysts to cover all shifts, costing upwards of $500,000 to $1,000,000 annually. Vigilense AI provides the same coverage for a fraction of that cost by automating the triage and investigation layers.

What features should midsize businesses look for in MDR?

Look for zero ingestion fees, the ability to keep data in your own infrastructure (S3/Snowflake), 24/7 automated triage, and native integration with your existing identity and endpoint tools.

What are the benefits of using AI in cyber security?

AI eliminates alert fatigue, provides instant correlation across disparate data sources, offers 24/7 monitoring without human shifts, and can execute containment actions at machine speed to stop ransomware before it spreads.

Does Vigilense AI work with Snowflake and BigQuery?

Yes. Vigilense is built to query data directly from Snowflake, BigQuery, Elasticsearch, and S3. This means you don't have to pay to move your logs to a vendor's cloud.

Can I use Vigilense if I already have an internal IT team?

Absolutely. Vigilense acts as a force multiplier for your IT team, handling the "boring" work of alert triage and only alerting your team when a verified threat requires human intervention.

Is AI security better than a human SOC?

AI is better at scale and speed, it can analyze millions of events per second. However, the best approach is "Human-in-the-loop," where AI handles the heavy lifting and humans make the final strategic decisions on critical incidents.

How long does it take to deploy Vigilense AI?

Vigilense can be live in days, not months. Since we don't require massive data migrations or complex hardware installs, the connection process is straightforward and rapid.
