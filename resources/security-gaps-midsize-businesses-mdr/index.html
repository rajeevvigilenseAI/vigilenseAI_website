How to Close Your Security Gaps in 5 Steps

Transitioning from a reactive security posture to a proactive MDR model doesn't have to be a multi-year project. Here is the blueprint for rapid protection.

Step 1: Audit Your Existing Data Sources

Identify where your critical logs and telemetry live. Most midsize businesses have data scattered across cloud providers (AWS, Azure), identity providers (Okta), and endpoints.

  • Map all S3 buckets and cloud storage.
  • List your primary identity and network log sources.

Step 2: Connect Vigilense to Your Infrastructure

Instead of moving your data to a new cloud, connect Vigilense directly to your existing Snowflake, BigQuery, or OpenSearch clusters. This maintains data sovereignty and cuts costs.

  • Configure read-only access for the AI engine.
  • Verify connection to 50+ integrated threat intel sources.

Step 3: Enable AI Threat Triage

Let the AI begin the heavy lifting of alert correlation. Every anomaly is investigated automatically, reducing the "noise" that typically overwhelms small IT teams.

  • Set baseline behavior patterns for your environment.
  • Allow AI to correlate identity, network, and endpoint data.

Step 4: Configure Automated Containment

Define what happens when a high-confidence threat is detected. You can set the system to automatically isolate an infected laptop or disable a compromised user account in seconds.

  • Define "Human in the Loop" thresholds for critical systems.
  • Test automated ticket creation in your existing workflow.

Step 5: Review Compliance & Audit Trails

Use the full audit trail generated by the MDR process to satisfy regulatory requirements. Vigilense provides transparent reporting on every investigation performed by the AI.

  • Generate monthly executive reports for stakeholders.
  • Maintain logs in your own environment for long-term compliance.

Frequently Asked Questions

What features should midsize businesses look for in an MDR?

You should prioritize AI-driven triage, 24/7 human-in-the-loop oversight, and a "bring your own data" model that avoids ingestion fees. Total visibility across cloud and on-prem is non-negotiable.

Which MDR tool is the fastest to deploy for teams?

Vigilense AI is designed to live in days, not months. Because it queries data where it lives, there is no massive data migration phase, allowing you to start detecting threats almost immediately.

How much does a midsize MDR solution cost?

While traditional providers charge per gigabyte of data, Vigilense offers a predictable model with zero ingestion fees. This typically saves midsize businesses 40-60% on their total security spend.

What are the benefits of using AI in threat detection?

AI can process millions of events per second, correlating identity shifts with network anomalies that a human would miss. This reduces the time to detect (MTTD) from months to minutes.

Why do attackers target businesses with fewer than 1,000 employees?

Attackers view midsize businesses as "soft targets" because they often have valuable data but lack the 24/7 monitoring capabilities of a Fortune 500 company.

Can I keep my data in my own Snowflake or S3?

Yes. Vigilense was built on the principle that your data stays yours. We query your existing infrastructure directly, ensuring you maintain full control and ownership.
