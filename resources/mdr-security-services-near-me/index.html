How to Deploy MDR Security Services (5 Steps)

Step 1: Audit Your Existing Data Sources

Identify where your critical logs and telemetry reside. This includes endpoints, identity providers (Okta, Azure AD), and cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, Azure).

Action items:
  • Map all cloud storage locations (S3, BigQuery, etc.).
  • List all active identity and endpoint security tools.

Step 2: Connect Your Infrastructure Without Data Movement

Instead of setting up costly ETL pipelines, connect Vigilense directly to your data lakes. Our AI queries the data where it lives, ensuring sovereignty and cost-savings.

Action items:
  • Grant read-only access to your data warehouse.
  • Verify connection latency across regional nodes.

Step 3: Configure AI Triage and Investigation Rules

Set the baseline for what constitutes an anomaly in your specific environment. The AI learns your "normal" to better identify "abnormal" behavior.

Action items:
  • Define high-priority assets and users.
  • Select from 50+ pre-built threat intelligence sources.

Step 4: Establish Automated Containment Protocols

Determine which threats should be handled automatically (like isolating an infected laptop) and which require human approval before action is taken.

Action items:
  • Set up notification webhooks for your IT team.
  • Test automated containment on a non-critical segment.

Step 5: Launch 24/7 Monitoring and Human-in-the-Loop

Go live with full 24/7 coverage. Our AI handles the bulk of the triage, while security experts remain available for complex investigations and strategic response.

Action items:
  • Conduct a final workflow walk-through.
  • Monitor the real-time dashboard for initial detections.
