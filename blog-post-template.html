Introducing the Sovereign SOC: Why We Built Vigilense AI

Feb 10, 2026 · 6 min read · By Raj Choudhary

We built Vigilense AI because traditional SIEMs force you to trade control for capability. The Sovereign SOC gives you both: BYODb architecture that queries your data in place, and an AI analyst that never sleeps.

The Problem with Traditional SIEMs

Traditional SIEMs require you to ship all your security data to their infrastructure. This creates data lock-in, escalating costs, and compliance headaches. You're paying $0.88 for every gigabyte your SIEM looks at -- not analyzes, not protects, just indexes.

500GB/day? That's $160K/year to build a search engine over your own data.

What Makes the Sovereign SOC Different

The Sovereign SOC flips this model: your data stays where it is, and we bring the intelligence to you.

BYODb Architecture

Your telemetry stays in your databases. ElasticSearch, Snowflake, BigQuery, S3 -- we query in place. Zero lock-in. Zero per-GB licensing. Zero rehydration tax. Our detection engine queries your data where it already lives.

Autonomous AI SOC

An AI-powered analyst that never sleeps and never suffers alert fatigue. It learns from past behaviors, retains organizational context, and works every alert 24/7/365. What used to take your team 4 hours to triage, investigate, and resolve now happens in seconds.

"Your security should never be someone else's leverage."

MTTR drops from hours to moments so your human analysts focus on high-value threats, not false positives and BAU noise.

Why Sovereignty Matters

We built Vigilense AI so you never have to trade control for capability. Your data is yours. Your context is yours. Your security operations are yours.

The Sovereign SOC gives you:

  • Your Data: BYODb -- query in place, zero lock-in
  • Your Context: Organizational learning that lives in your environment
  • Your Analyst: Autonomous AI SOC that works 24/7/365

What's Next

We're just getting started. The Sovereign SOC is the foundation for a new era of security operations -- one where you have control, capability, and cost efficiency all at once.
